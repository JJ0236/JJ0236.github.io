About Me

Get to know who I am and what inspires my passion for innovation and the outdoors

Who I Am

Hello! I am Josh Hicks, a creative and curious individual with a deep passion for technology, innovation, and the natural world. I love exploring how modern tech can enhance our experiences of nature and I strive to bring both my technical skills and my love for the outdoors into every project I undertake. I am also an Eagle Scout, an experience that taught me valuable lessons about leadership, perseverance, and the beauty of nature.

My Academic Journey

I am currently pursuing an Information Systems degree at the University of Arkansas. My academic journey is all about finding creative solutions that merge technology with real-world challenges, including sustainable and nature-friendly innovations that help us live in harmony with our environment.

Interests and Hobbies

When I am not immersed in technology, I enjoy exploring the outdoors, hiking, and connecting with nature. My interests span from personal tech projects and robotics to nature photography and environmental conservation. I believe that spending time outdoors fuels my creativity and inspires my work in technology.

What Drives Me

I am driven by a passion to merge innovation with nature, creating solutions that enhance our lives while respecting our planet. I continually seek opportunities to learn and grow, pushing the boundaries of technology, all while nurturing my love for the outdoors. This balance inspires me to make a positive impact on the world.
