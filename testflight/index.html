Your Journey

Five moments
you won’t forget.

01

Harrison, AR

Lift off from the valley

Your pilot briefs you beside the aircraft on a quiet private pad north of town. The valley floor drops away — and suddenly the entire Buffalo River watershed opens up beneath you.

02

Boxley Valley

Elk meadows and the upper Buffalo

A patchwork of farmland and open meadow where elk herds roam freely. We follow the upper Buffalo River while it’s still cold and clear, winding through the valley below.

03

Hawksbill Crag — Whitaker Point

Circle Arkansas’s most iconic overlook

The most photographed rock formation in Arkansas juts from a 2,000-foot ridge. From the air you see its true scale — and the river bend 1,000 feet directly below it.

04

Turner Bend — Lost Valley

Drop into a hidden canyon

A slot canyon most people never find on foot. We bank low over Clark Creek’s natural bridge, then circle back over the Blue Hole — a spring-fed swimming hole the color of jade.

05

Ouachita Foothills — Crystal Mines

Land at the crystal mines, then ride the sunset home

We set down at a working quartz formation. You’re handed a pick — twenty minutes of digging, then back in the air for the flight home as the ridgelines go gold behind you.
