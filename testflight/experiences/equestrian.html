◆ Trail Riding

Equestrian & Ride Therapy

Show-caliber stables and trails only horses can reach.

Some of the finest trail riding in Arkansas hides in valleys no road touches — and a helicopter is the only graceful way in.

Lift off from Thaden Field in Bentonville and follow the ridgelines south. In under twenty minutes the Ozark canopy opens onto a private farm just outside Fayetteville — roughly nineteen miles from the field as the helicopter flies, a world away from the highway version of the trip.

Jasper Springs Ranch keeps show-caliber horses matched to every level of rider. Down the road, Parkerman Stables rounds out the day with a therapeutic-horsemanship program whose quiet, hands-on method has helped riders of all abilities find their seat and their confidence. Together the two operations give this experience a range few trail days can claim: sporting and restorative, fast and slow, ridge-top views and valley-bottom quiet.

Routes run through terrain only horses (and helicopters) reach — limestone ridge trails, creek-bottom paths, and long grassy swales the Ozarks keep off the map. Choose a sunrise ride that catches the mist on the hills, a half-day out-and-back, or a full day that ends around a cookout fire with lunch laid out before the flight home.

What’s included

  • Round-trip helicopter transfer from Thaden Field, Bentonville
  • Horse matched to your experience level on arrival
  • Ridge & valley trail routes no road reaches
  • Optional therapeutic-horsemanship session at Parkerman Stables
  • Sunrise, half-day, and full-day ride options
  • Cookout lunch on full-day rides

How the day flies

Four steps, one unforgettable afternoon.

Depart Thaden Field

Meet your pilot in Bentonville and lift off straight over the ridgelines — no traffic, no shuttle, no waiting.

Land at the farm

Touch down at the ranch near Fayetteville where the horses are already groomed and your guide is ready to introduce you.

Matched to your horse

Whether you’re a seasoned rider or first-timer, the team pairs you to the right horse and sets the pace to match.

Ride the backcountry

Follow ridge and valley trails that no road reaches, with an optional ride-therapy session and a cookout lunch before the flight home.
