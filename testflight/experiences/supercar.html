Supercar & Heritage Rentals

Lamborghini, Ferrari, classic muscle — and the mountain roads to match.

The Ozark two-lanes were practically designed for a supercar — and a helicopter is the only fitting way to arrive.

Lift off from Thaden Field in Bentonville and trade the interstate for a low-altitude flight over rolling ridge country. In twenty minutes you’re on the ground at a private collection — a quiet property where the fleet is already out in the sun, waiting to be introduced.

A specialist walks you through each car: the provenance, the feel, what to expect from the first corner. Then the keys are yours. A curated driving route threads through the best of the Arkansas highlands — sweeping switchbacks, long valley straights, and pull-offs at overlooks that belong on a different continent. A support vehicle follows at a respectful distance so you never feel unsupported.

Half-day rentals work beautifully for a morning escape; full-day options give you the miles to really settle into the car. Bring a co-pilot or go solo — both configurations are available. The helicopter is waiting when the drive is done.

What’s included

  • Round-trip helicopter transfer from Thaden Field, Bentonville
  • Choice of supercar or classic American muscle from a private collection
  • Curated mountain driving route through the Ozarks
  • Professional briefing & dedicated support vehicle
  • Scenic overlook stops along the route
  • Half-day and full-day rental options available

How the day flies

Four steps, one unforgettable afternoon.

Depart Thaden Field

Meet your pilot in Bentonville and lift off over the Ozark ridgelines — no highway, no wait, no crowd.

Meet the fleet

Touch down at the private collection and walk the cars with a specialist who knows every one of them.

Take the keys

Choose your car, get the briefing, and pull out onto a curated route built around the best roads in the region.

Carve the mountain roads

Sweeping bends, long valley straights, overlook pull-offs — then back to the pad and home before dark.
